[English] Data Collection & Usage

Our applications (such as GEKINATU VIDEOS, Sokone, and SimpleAnything) do not collect personal information for transmission to external servers. All data remains exclusively on your own device or local machine.

TikTok API Integration

GEKINATU VIDEOS integrates with the TikTok Content Posting API to facilitate automated content management. We only access your account through access tokens generated after your explicit authorization.

Data Privacy: We do not store, share, or sell your videos, photos, or profile data. All authentication tokens and promotional assets are stored locally on your machine and are never transmitted to our own servers or any third parties.
